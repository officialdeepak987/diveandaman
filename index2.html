    About Dive Andaman

    Scuba Diving in Andaman Islands

    Located in the magnificent Bay of Bengal, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands is simply the most gorgeous destination in India. This union territory is known for its breathtaking aquatic world. Dive Andaman is an elite PADI center based in Swaraj Dweep (previously Havelock Island) that caters to every diver’s need with professionalism and safety. It is a centrally-located dive shop with modern scuba diving equipment and expert dive masters whom you can trust.
